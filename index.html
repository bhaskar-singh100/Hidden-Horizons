    Valley of Flowers

    Let's find some beautiful place to get lost.


    Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh

    It is known for its lush landscapes and vibrant rice fields, and it is home to the
    Apatani tribe and offers a serene escape in the northeastern part of India.


    Gokarna, Karnataka

    It is often referred to as a more peaceful alternative to Goa, Gokarna boasts
    pristine beaches, scenic landscapes, and sacred temples.


    Khajjiar, Himachal Pradesh

    It is a picturesque meadow captivates with its lush landscapes, dense deodar forests,
    and breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains, providing a perfect escape for
    nature lovers and adventure seekers.


    Gurez Valley, Jammu and Kashmir

    It is Known for its breathtaking vistas, charming villages, and the majestic
    Habba Khatoon Peak. It offers a tranquil heaven for those seeking serenity in
    the lap of the Himalayas.


    Majuli, Assam

    It is the largest river island in the world, and is located in the Brahmaputra River.
    It offers a unique blend of rich cultural heritage, serene landscapes,
    and diverse flora and fauna.


    Chopta, Uttarakhand

    It is a small region of trek and evergreen forest area, a part of himalayas wildlife sanctuary
    located in Uttarakhand and a base for trekking to Tungnath.
